# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Leadroom a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Positive behaviour includes empathy, respectful disagreement, constructive feedback, accepting responsibility, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behaviour includes sexualised language or imagery, personal attacks, harassment, publishing private information without permission, and other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and whenever an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behaviour privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Security vulnerabilities should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.
